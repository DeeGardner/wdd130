about

We live in an unprecedented time, more information is finger-taps away than was available in all of the last 3,000 years combined. The big problem we face isn’t where is it all? It’s how, and what matters most, because it’s all available. Packets answers these questions and more, we are a centralized hub where important information is organized and summarized in easy to access and understand chunks or “packets”. Essentially we provide access to information that’s clear, concise, and appropriate on a broad range of topics. People who really want to learn tend to end up here because this isn’t your normal research resource. Our mission is to change the way people study and learn by making the process easy and accessible,

breezy, simple, and accurate. Answers here are clear, organized, concise, and true, delivered without distraction or fluff. Learn, instead of intake. Packets is run by a small team based in the United States of America, committed to inspiring real learning. Packets was founded to fight the confusion that makes learning so hard, in a world where information is so accessible. Our content is provided by qualified volunteers and each article is checked thoroughly before publication. We strive to never be a distraction to you while learning here and so refuse to show advertisements or other kinds of promotion. Instead we rely on donations from truth loving people to provide you with the quality content users come to expect here. (Click Here)
