Who We Are

The Dry Oar Rafting company is famous for unique and refreshing experiences, our name is synonymous with passion and drive to improve. Where most find something that works and stick with it, we strive to improve with every experience we provide. Our dedicated team puts you first in every circumstance and has years of experience rafting rivers and guiding river trips. Based in Northern Idaho for more than 90 years, our company knows the rivers, and we know how to make every trip unforgettable.
