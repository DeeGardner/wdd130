Site Name

Dry Oar Rafting Co.

Site Purpose with Goals

To provide a face for the Dry Oar Rafting Company, information, and resources for booking a rafting trip. It should also be a useful marketing tool. It should provide trip expectations and preparation instructions including directions. It should also include testimonials and experiences. It should include a small merchandise store.

Target Audience

What will weather look like during my trip? How can we ensure appropriate weather?

Is it worth booking a rafting trip, and how? How can we make it easy and attracrtive?

How much is it. How can we justify the cost?

Is it simple to do? How can we make the process smooth?

How do I prepare for a trip or reference my booked trip’s information. How can we make information accessible?

If customers are looking to book a trip, what will sell them?

How can we target a middle age audience which includes their family.

How can we appear honest. What promises or commitments do we need to make?

How can we prepare clients effectively. How can we effectively provide information and expectations, as well as advice? Would a help forum be worthwhile? Or a FAQ page?

How can we gather testimonials and experiences from customers? We need a post survey likely.
